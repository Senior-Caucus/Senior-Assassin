Rules and Guidelines

Please read the following rules and guidelines carefully. Failure to follow any of these rules will result in immediate disqualification.

  1. Where/How can someone be assassinated?
    1. Assassinations cannot occur in class, staircases, escalators, bathrooms, or locker rooms.
    2. Assassinations can occur outside of PE class while waiting for class to start, and outside of school.
    3. Assassinations cannot occur on subway platforms.
    4. To assassinate, gently tap a spoon on the target's shoulder (plastic spoons from the cafeteria are allowed; no other utensils).
  2. Safety Items
    1. There are no safety items in this game mode.
  3. Evidence Submission
    1. Video evidence must clearly show the kill and the target's face.
    2. Video evidence must be submitted within 24 hours of the kill.
    3. You cannot submit evidence for the same target more than once.
  4. Hearts System
    1. Each player starts with a certain number of hearts.
    2. If you are eliminated, you lose a heart.
    3. If you eliminate someone, you gain a heart. However, if you have 0, 1/3, or 2/3 hearts, you only gain 1/3 of a heart.
    4. If your hearts reach 0, you are eliminated from the game.

Failure to comply: If a player fails to follow any of the rules and guidelines listed above, they will be immediately disqualified from playing Senior Assassin.
