Rules and Instructions v3

Please read the following rules and instructions for this specific game mode carefully.

TLDR: Get as many spoon taps as possible before Friday night; one spoon tap = one heart; you lose at most one heart if you are tapped; the people with the most hearts get into the final round.

No running is allowed (in school). No safety items this week. You don't need to wait for your video to be approved anymore. Good luck.

  1. Where/How can someone be assassinated?
    1. Assassinations cannot occur in class, staircases, escalators, bathrooms, or locker rooms.
    2. Assassinations can occur outside of PE class while waiting for class to start, and outside of school.
    3. Assassinations cannot occur on subway platforms.
    4. To assassinate, gently tap a spoon on the target's shoulder (plastic spoons from the cafeteria are allowed; no other utensils).
    5. If you are in school, you are not allowed to run. You may submit evidence of your target running as evidence.
  2. Safety Items
    1. There are no safety items in this game mode.
  3. Evidence Submission
    1. Video evidence must clearly show the elimination and the target's face.
    2. You cannot submit evidence for the same target more than once.
    3. We will review all evidence during the weekend.
  4. Hearts System
    1. Each player starts with 3 hearts if previously alive or 0 hearts if previously eliminated.
    2. If you eliminate someone, you gain a heart. However, if you have 0, 1/3, or 2/3 of a heart, you only gain 1/3 of a heart.
    3. Each time you are eliminated, you lose a heart. If you have 1/3 or 2/3 of a heart, you lose your partial heart.
    4. If you have 0 hearts, you cannot lose any more hearts.
  5. Winning
    1. The final round will be held on Monday, June 16th.
    2. The five people with the most hearts by Friday night will be automatically admitted to the final round.
    3. Up to five additional random people will be selected to participate in the final round.

Failure to comply: If a player fails to follow any of the rules and instructions listed above, they will be immediately disqualified from playing Senior Assassin.
